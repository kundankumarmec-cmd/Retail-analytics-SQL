# Retail analytics:
Retail analytics is performed to analyze sales performance, customer segments, and inventory management. By identifying high or low-performing products and understanding customer behavior, businesses can make informed decisions to drive growth.

# Purpose:
To encounter challenges in understanding its Sales performance,Customer segment, and Inventory management.
Though comprehensive data analysis approach, the company aims to identify high or low sales products, effectively segment its customer base,
and analyze customer behavior to enhance marketing strategies, inventory decisions, and overall customer experience.

# Tech stack:
The Retail analytics was performed by using following tools and technologies:
MYSQL: Utilized for data cleaning and exploratory data analysis, to identify high and low sales products,to segment customers,to analyze customer behavior and loyalty.
Excel:To import data in SQL
File formate: .sql to interact with and manage relational databases, .csv to store tabular data.

# Data source:
Coding Ninjas Data analytics bootcamp.

# Business Problem:
The retail company has observed stagnant growth and declining customer engagement metrics over the past quarters.
Initial assessments indicate potential issues in product performance variability, ineffective customer segmentation, and
lack of insights into customer purchasing behavior.
The company seeks to leverage its sales transaction data, customer profiles, and product inventory
information to address the following key business problems:

• Product Performance Variability: Identifying which products are performing well in terms of sales and which are not.
 This insight is crucial for inventory management and marketing focus.
• Customer Segmentation: The company lacks a clear understanding of its customer base segmentation.
  Effective segmentation is essential for targeted marketing and enhancing customer satisfaction.
• Customer Behaviour Analysis: Understanding patterns in customer behavior,including repeat purchases and loyalty indicators,
  is critical for tailoring customer engagement strategies and improving retention rates.

# Objectives:
• To utilize SQL queries for data cleaning and exploratory data analysis to ensure data quality and gain initial insights.
• To identify high and low sales products to optimize inventory and tailor marketing efforts.
• To segment customers based on their purchasing behavior for targeted marketing campaigns.
• To analyze customer behavior for insights on repeat purchases and loyalty,informing customer retention strategies.
  
# Business Impact & Insights:
•Company needed cleaner data and actionable insights to improve sales, marketing, and retention.
•Used SQL and analytics to clean datasets (+15% accuracy, –20% errors), identify sales trends, segment customers, and analyze loyalty drivers.
•Boosted revenue by 10%, cut inventory by 12%, lifted campaign engagement by 25%, improved conversions by 18%, raised CLV(customer lifetime value) by 22%, and increased repeat purchases by 15%.
